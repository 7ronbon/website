Data Coordinator

July - Aug 2023 | NYC Department of Social Services

I am passionate about helping communities when i am given the oppurtunies to. As a data coordinator, I was given the chance to help others through a bigger means. By sorting through a list of 400+ shelters and assessing the resources they have on site and identifying what they lack, it lets the NYC DOHS know which shelters are in need of more resources so they know how to help their community better.

    key notes:
  • Conducting outreach to 400+ overall shelters to assess on-site clinical services.
  • Maintained, monitored, and surveyed databases through routine QA on redcap.
  • Identifying shelters with minimal resources to ensure shelters are appropriately staffed
